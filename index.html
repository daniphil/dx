About Me

Where I started. The origin storyI've always been fascinated by people—what makes us tick, click, and occasionally rage-quit. That curiosity pulled me into the wild west of the early internet, where I got my start with one of the OG internet service providers. That’s where I first tangled with web design, information architecture, and the delicate art of getting humans to not smash their keyboards. Spoiler: I didn’t stop there.

Foundational Expertise & Career EvolutionAt Synopsys, I spent six years shaping SolvNet into an award-winning support portal (IEEE Best-in-Class, no big deal). It sharpened my B2B chops and made me fluent in the fine print of self-service design. Then came Intel—a decade of diving deep into developer experience, AI systems, and enterprise tools.

What Gets Me Up in the Morning. I'm driven by the challenge of uncovering the “why” behind user behavior—what motivates people, what frustrates them, and how those insights can shape better experiences. I'm hooked on the "why." Why do users ghost your product? Why do developers curse at your interface? Why do people click everything except the big obvious button? Finding those answers fuels my work—and makes your product better. I bridge user needs with business goals so you don't have to.

What value I add.I specialize in translating research into actionable strategy. By applying mixed-method research, journey mapping, and usability testing, I help product teams build experiences that are intuitive, efficient, and scalable. My work improves onboarding, streamlines workflows, and boosts adoption—bridging the gap between user needs and product goals. I bring clarity, structure, and a user-first mindset to every stage of development.

StorytellingResearch is only impactful when it resonates. I craft stories that bring user experiences to life, getting teams aligned and amped to build the right thing.

Collaboration & InfluenceI play well with product, engineering, and design. Through mentoring, facilitation, and the occasional strategic post-it note, I help turn insights into “aha” moments.
